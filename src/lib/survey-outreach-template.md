# Survey Outreach Email Template

Use this template when emailing contractor association chapter executives.
Personalise `[Name]`, `[Association]`, and `[Chapter/Region]` before sending.

---

**Subject:** Free Data Partnership — ConstructAIQ Quarterly Survey

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because [Association] members have valuable market intelligence
that the broader industry would benefit from.

ConstructAIQ is a free construction intelligence platform (constructaiq.trade)
that publishes market data and AI forecasts for the entire US construction sector.
We are building a quarterly survey of construction professionals — the Backlog
Outlook Index, Margin Expectation Index, and Labor Availability Index.

We are asking [Association] to share the survey link with members quarterly.
In return:

- Members get early access to results (48 hours before public)
- [Association] gets attribution on every results publication
- The aggregate results are free and shareable
- No individual responses are ever published

The survey takes 90 seconds. The Q2 2025 survey is open now:
constructaiq.trade/survey

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss?

---

## Follow-up cadence

1. **Day 0** — send initial email above
2. **Day 7** — one follow-up if no reply: "Wanted to make sure this didn't get buried…"
3. **Day 14** — final: short note with survey link + respondent count so far ("142 GCs have already responded")

## Target contacts

Priority associations for initial outreach:

| Association | Contact type | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| AGC National | Member comms / VP membership | Best reach for large GCs |
| AGC chapters (state-level) | Chapter executive directors | More responsive; 50+ chapters |
| ABC (Associated Builders and Contractors) | Marketing / member services | Open shop; national + chapters |
| NRCA | VP Communications | Roofing specialty |
| NECA | Member engagement | Electrical specialty |
| SMACNA | Marketing director | HVAC/sheet metal |

## Attribution wording for results publications

Include the following line in results pages when an association has
agreed to distribute:

> Respondents recruited in partnership with [Association Name].
